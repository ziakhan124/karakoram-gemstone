Our Story

From The Legendary Karakoram Mountains

KaraKoram Gems was founded with a passion for sharing the natural treasures hidden within the majestic mountains of Gilgit-Baltistan, Pakistan.

For generations, our family has been connected with gemstone sourcing and trading. Our experience allows us to identify exceptional natural gemstones, rare mineral specimens, and quality rough materials.

We believe every gemstone carries a story — a journey from deep within the earth to collectors, jewelers, and designers around the world.

Our Mission

Our mission is to provide authentic, naturally sourced gemstones while building trusted relationships with collectors, jewelers, and wholesale partners worldwide.
