Our Story

Gemstones with origin, character, and trust.

We connect collectors, jewelers, and trade buyers with natural stones from the legendary Karakoram region.

Gilgit-Baltistan

From mountain deposits to global collections.

KaraKoram Gems was founded with a passion for sharing the natural treasures hidden within the mountains of Gilgit-Baltistan, Pakistan.

For generations, our family has been connected with gemstone sourcing and trading. That experience helps us identify natural gemstones, rare mineral specimens, and quality rough material for different buyers and uses.

We believe every gemstone carries a journey from deep within the earth to collectors, jewelers, designers, and dealers around the world.

Every selection starts with natural structure, color, and authenticity.
